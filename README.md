## Frequently Asked Questions ##

### Is Hero AI Blogger really free? ###

Yes! You can create a free account directly inside the plugin and claim 20,000 free tokens every month, which is enough for multiple full-length articles. No credit card is required to get started.

### Do I need my own OpenAI or other AI API key? ###

No. Hero AI Blogger connects to our secure AI service using a free API key you claim right from your dashboard in about 30 seconds. The heavy AI processing happens on our servers, so your site's performance isn't affected.

### How does the auto-blogging work? ###

You create a "Campaign" based on your target keywords or topics, choose how many posts you want, and set a publishing schedule. The plugin then automatically generates, formats, and publishes posts in the background using the native WordPress cron system.

### Can I control when posts are published? ###

Yes. With Smart Scheduling (drip-feed) you can avoid publishing everything at once. Tell the AI to publish a new post every few days or weeks to keep your content fresh and your site regularly crawled by search engines.

### Will the posts include images? ###

Yes. The plugin automatically fetches and attaches royalty-free featured images from Unsplash, Pixabay and other platforms to your generated posts, so each article is publish-ready with visuals. We do not use any copyrighted images, so you can be confident that your content is safe and compliant.

### Can I match the content to my brand voice? ###

Absolutely. The Custom Persona Engine lets you train the AI on your target audience, site purpose, and preferred tone, so every generated article sounds like you.

### Is the generated content safe for my brand? ###

Yes. Built-in AI safety controls automatically block harassment, hate speech, and other harmful or dangerous content, helping you maintain a professional and trustworthy blog.

### Are posts published automatically or saved as drafts? ###

You decide. Configure your campaign to publish posts automatically on your chosen schedule, or review them before they go live.

### Does this plugin slow down my website? ###

No. The AI processing runs on our secure remote servers rather than your own hosting, so your site's speed and performance are not impacted by content generation.
